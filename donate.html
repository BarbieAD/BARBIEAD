Donation Information

Your generous donations will help fund our mission and the goals we set for the community. All donations will be held by the main office until they are distributed for the intended purposes.

How to Donate

Donations can be made through several methods:

  • Online payments via PayPal
  • Cash donations delivered to the main office
  • Check payments made out to our organization

For more information on how to donate, feel free to contact us via email or phone.

Toy Drop Off

Toy donations can be dropped off at the main office. Please ensure that the toys are in good condition, and we greatly appreciate your contributions to making the holidays brighter for those in need.
